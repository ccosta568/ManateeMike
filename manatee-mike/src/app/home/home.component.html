Adventurer of the Florida Gulf

Michael Costa was born in Massachusetts and grew up on the outskirts of Boston. For most of his young life Mike enjoyed playing football, performing in plays and being the lead singer of a band. He later attended Emerson College in Boston where he met his future wife “The Godmother of Horror” Elaine Pascale. He then had a son and daughter named Christopher and Sierra, and moved down to Cape Cod.

He devoted his time and energy to kids in need all around him. Whether teaching in a classroom, or coaching a team, he was always available to help. Today Michael and Elaine reside in Florida where they go manatee and dolphin sightseeing regularly on their paddleboards together. Mike also enjoys a good football game in his down time. He is the 2021 Armchair Fantasy Football League SuperBowl Champ!
